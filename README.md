Stay on top of every pitch, swing, and final score without losing focus on your work.
The MLB Scoreboard Add-on delivers live MLB game updates, stats, and results directly in your Chrome browser — no need to keep switching tabs.

🔗 Get it on the Chrome Web Store

✨ Features

🟢 Live Game Feed – See real-time scores and play-by-play updates.

📊 Detailed Game Data – View lineups, pitching matchups, player stats, and more.

🖼 Visual Scoreboard – A clean and compact design that keeps everything in one glance.

⏱ Stay Productive – Track your favorite teams while keeping your workflow uninterrupted.

🔔 Instant Access – Open the extension anytime from your browser toolbar.
